## Recap

No order, with replacement choose 4 out of n (sample table)  
- extreme cases:  
	k=0, $\binom{n-1}{0}=0$  
	k=1, $\binom{n}{1}=n$  
- simplest nontrivial:
	n=2, $\binom{k+1}{k}=\binom{k+1}{1}=k+1$
